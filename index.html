      Controls

      • Move: WASD / Arrows
      • Aim: Mouse cursor
      • Cast spell: Enter (load fireball)
      • Fire: Space / Click (10 MP, needs loaded spell)
      • Open door / chest: E (when prompted)
      • Dash: Shift — speed burst, 0.35s invuln, 1.2s cooldown (bar under HP)
      • Sound: SND button (top-right) toggles mute
      • Inventory: Tab
      • Pause: Esc

      Goals

      • Type the displayed sentence in time to load a fireball into the hotbar.
      • Each fired fireball consumes one charge from the hotbar plus 10 MP.
      • Clear every room. Boss kills advance the level.
      • Level 3+ runs a shrinking timer — survive it.
      • Pink ranged archers stop at distance and fire every 2s — dodge their shots.
